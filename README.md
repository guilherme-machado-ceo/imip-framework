## What is IMIP?

The **Multiple Integrated Instantiation of Thought (IMIP)** framework
is a formal model in which a single cognitive subject operates
simultaneously across scientific, artistic, and entrepreneurial
instances — in parallel or in series — within a continuous flow of
research, study, creation, teaching, and economic production.

Its central contribution is architectural: IMIP proposes that the
**alignment problem in AGI may be resolved not by external restriction
but by constitutional instance design** — each cognitive instance
constituted such that certain operations are structurally impossible,
not merely prohibited.
